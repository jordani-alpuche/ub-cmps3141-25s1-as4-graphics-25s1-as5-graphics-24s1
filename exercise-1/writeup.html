Exercise 1: Typography Improvements

  • Font: I switched the headings to a Serif font (Georgia) to create a better contrast against the Sans-Serif body text.
  • Visual Hierarchy: I increased the h1 size to 2.5rem and h2 to 2rem to ensure the page title and section headers clearly stand out from the content.
  • Measure (Line Length): I updated the paragraph width to 65ch (65 characters), which is widely considered the optimal line length for human reading comfort.
